About Us



Saelink Law focuses on providing high-quality and comprehensive legal service and solutions for increasingly complex and novel issues of IP, antitrust, and corporation law, to clients from all over the world. Saelink Law is specialized in handling patent, trade secret, IP licensing issues of advanced technology as well as dispute resolutions, compliance matters and risks control, and cross-border negotiations of antitrust,competition, data security under emerging business models. We have successfully assisted numerous leading transnational groups, unicorn companies, and high-tech enterprises to win their cases in traditional and updated IP and antitrust cases. Our service covers Semiconductors, Wireless Communication, LED, Materials Chemical, Medicine, Chemical Industry, High-end Manufacture, Medical Apparatus and Instruments, TMT, Internet, Artificial Intelligence, Sports and Entertainment, etc.



Our team consists of professionals with diverse and abundant practice experiences, with renowed experts who possess profound knowledge of IP law and litigation through more than a decade work experience in judging and handling thousands of cases; a well-known attorney who has worked on both scientific research and IP litigation for a long time, possessing dual qualifications of lawyer and patent agent, and attending a good deal of crucial cases in the Supreme Court; a famous litigator with extensive practical experience in international and domestic top law firms, specialized in combining technical knowledge and legal service; a prestigious expert with rich experience in judicial career and senior in-house legal counsel in a world-leading enterprise; and a notable patent agent who has practiced patent examination for more than 10 years and excelled at patent validity disputes. Our team also welcomes a number of professionals who graduated from top universities and possess intimate knowledge of law and experience in practice.



SAELINK LAW implements corporate system. We combine our partners and attorneys, basing on their respective experience and specialty, to form a team that can provide professional and efficient services for our clients. Our extensive experience in practice has mixed our professional legal skills with commercial awareness and judgment, so that we can assist our client to make and execute the business strategies that bring them the best commercial benefits. We also cooperate with external technical experts, examination agencies, survey and consulting companies, and PR companies, allocating resources basing on the needs of cases, for the purpose of bringing our clients the optimal outcome and sustainable competitive advantages.
