About Saelink

Saelink focuses on complicated IP, antitrust, and commercial disputes of corporations, specialized in the patents, trade secrets, licensing issues of cutting-edge technology, as well as the antitrust, competition, data security under the emerging business mode, and dispute resolutions, compliance matters and risks control, cross-border negotiations of commercial affairs and international trades.

Specialized in IP, competition law, commercial litigation and arbitration, our team consists of professionals with diverse backgrounds and plentiful experiences. Our team has a well-deserved reputation for professional attitude, specialized legal knowledge and extensive practical experiences.

Devoting to put our clients in the best and sustainable position, we offer cautious, efficient, professional and bespoke services to transnational corporations and major domestic enterprises, unicorn companies, and high-tech start-ups.
